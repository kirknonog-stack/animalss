Rabbit

Small herbivores with strong hind legs and long ears.

  • Species: Oryctolagus cuniculus
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Size: 12-20 inches long
  • Weight: 2-11 lbs
  • Life span: 8-12 years
  • Habitat: Meadows, woods, grasslands
  • Found: Worldwide
  • Can be pet: Yes

Trivia: Rabbits have nearly 360-degree vision to watch for predators.

Rat

Adaptable rodents found in cities, forests, and farms.

  • Species: Rattus
  • Diet: Omnivore
  • Size: 6-10 inches long
  • Weight: 7-18 ounces
  • Life span: 2-3 years
  • Habitat: Urban areas, fields
  • Found: Worldwide
  • Can be pet: Sometimes

Trivia: Rats are highly intelligent and can learn mazes and tricks.

Raccoon

Masked mammals known for their dexterous hands and curiosity.

  • Species: Procyon lotor
  • Diet: Omnivore
  • Size: 16-28 inches long
  • Weight: 10-30 lbs
  • Life span: 2-5 years
  • Habitat: Forests, cities
  • Found: North America
  • Can be pet: Sometimes

Trivia: Raccoons use their front paws like hands and can open jars and latches.

Rhino

Large herbivores with one or two horns on their noses.

  • Species: Rhinocerotidae
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Size: 5-6 ft tall
  • Weight: 1,800-5,000 lbs
  • Life span: 35-50 years
  • Habitat: Grasslands, savannas
  • Found: Africa, Asia
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Rhinos have poor eyesight but an excellent sense of smell.

Reindeer

Cold-adapted deer known for migrating across Arctic regions.

  • Species: Rangifer tarandus
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Size: 3-5 ft tall
  • Weight: 150-400 lbs
  • Life span: 10-15 years
  • Habitat: Tundra, boreal forests
  • Found: Arctic regions
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Both male and female reindeer grow antlers.

Rooster

Male chickens famous for crowing at dawn.

  • Species: Gallus gallus domesticus
  • Diet: Omnivore
  • Size: 2-3 ft tall
  • Weight: 5-10 lbs
  • Life span: 5-8 years
  • Habitat: Farms, backyards
  • Found: Worldwide
  • Can be pet: Yes

Trivia: Roosters protect their flock and may sound an alarm if threatened.
