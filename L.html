Lion

Majestic big cats known as kings of the savanna.

  • Species: Panthera leo
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 3.5-4 ft tall
  • Weight: 330-550 lbs
  • Life span: 10-14 years
  • Habitat: Grasslands, savannas
  • Found: Africa, India
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Lions are the only cats that live in groups called prides.

Leopard

Stealthy nocturnal cats with distinctive spotted fur.

  • Species: Panthera pardus
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 2-3 ft tall
  • Weight: 66-176 lbs
  • Life span: 12-17 years
  • Habitat: Forests, grasslands
  • Found: Africa, Asia
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Leopards often stash prey in trees to keep it safe from scavengers.

Llama

Domesticated camelids used for pack transport and wool.

  • Species: Lama glama
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Size: 4.5-5.5 ft tall
  • Weight: 250-450 lbs
  • Life span: 15-25 years
  • Habitat: Highlands, farms
  • Found: South America
  • Can be pet: Yes

Trivia: Llamas are very social animals and communicate with humming sounds.

Lobster

Marine crustaceans with powerful claws and long antennae.

  • Species: Nephropidae
  • Diet: Omnivore
  • Size: 8-24 inches long
  • Weight: 1-20 lbs
  • Life span: 20-50 years
  • Habitat: Ocean floors
  • Found: Worldwide oceans
  • Can be pet: Sometimes

Trivia: Lobsters can regenerate lost limbs over time.

Lizard

A diverse group of reptiles found in nearly every habitat.

  • Species: Squamata
  • Diet: Varies by species
  • Size: 1-6 ft long
  • Weight: 0.1 oz to 30 lbs
  • Life span: 1-20 years
  • Habitat: Forests, deserts, urban areas
  • Found: Worldwide
  • Can be pet: Sometimes

Trivia: Some lizards can detach their tails to distract predators.

Lynx

Medium-sized wild cats with tufted ears and padded paws for snowy terrain.

  • Species: Lynx lynx
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 2-3 ft tall
  • Weight: 40-66 lbs
  • Life span: 10-15 years
  • Habitat: Forests, mountains
  • Found: Europe, Asia, North America
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Lynx have large paws that act like snowshoes for walking on snow.
