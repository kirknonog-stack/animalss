Zebra

Striped equids native to African savannas.

  • Species: Equus quagga
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Size: 4-5 ft tall
  • Weight: 400-900 lbs
  • Life span: 20-30 years
  • Habitat: Grasslands, savannas
  • Found: Africa
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Zebra stripes are unique to each individual.

Zebu

Humped cattle from South Asia adapted to hot climates.

  • Species: Bos indicus
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Size: 4-5 ft tall
  • Weight: 600-1,500 lbs
  • Life span: 15-25 years
  • Habitat: Grasslands, farms
  • Found: South Asia, Africa
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Zebu are often used as draft animals and for dairy in tropical regions.

Zorilla

Striped African mammals also known as polecats.

  • Species: Ictonyx striatus
  • Diet: Omnivore
  • Size: 1.5-2 ft long
  • Weight: 4-7 lbs
  • Life span: 10-15 years
  • Habitat: Savannas, woodlands
  • Found: Africa
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Zorillas can spray a foul-smelling liquid when threatened.

Zander

Freshwater game fish found across Europe and Asia.

  • Species: Sander lucioperca
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 1-3 ft long
  • Weight: 2-20 lbs
  • Life span: 10-15 years
  • Habitat: Lakes, rivers
  • Found: Europe, Asia
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Zander are prized sport fish with sharp teeth and strong runs.

Zebra Shark

A gentle reef shark with dark stripes as a juvenile.

  • Species: Stegostoma fasciatum
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 5-8 ft long
  • Weight: 100-300 lbs
  • Life span: 25-30 years
  • Habitat: Coral reefs
  • Found: Indo-Pacific
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Zebra sharks are nocturnal and feed on shellfish and small fish.

Zonkey

A hybrid offspring of a zebra and a donkey.

  • Species: Hybrid
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Size: 4-5 ft tall
  • Weight: 500-900 lbs
  • Life span: 20-30 years
  • Habitat: Farms, sanctuaries
  • Found: Captivity
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Zonkeys inherit zebra stripes and donkey shape in varying patterns.
