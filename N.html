Newt

Small amphibians with smooth skin and a long tail.

  • Species: Triturus
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 4-7 inches long
  • Weight: 0.5-1 ounce
  • Life span: 6-14 years
  • Habitat: Ponds, woodlands
  • Found: Europe, Asia, Americas
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Newts can regenerate lost limbs and even parts of their heart.

Narwhal

Arctic whales famous for their long spiral tusks.

  • Species: Monodon monoceros
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 13-18 ft long
  • Weight: 1,800-3,500 lbs
  • Life span: 30-50 years
  • Habitat: Arctic waters
  • Found: Canada, Greenland, Russia
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: The narwhal’s tusk is actually a long canine tooth.

Nightingale

Songbirds best known for their beautiful night calls.

  • Species: Luscinia megarhynchos
  • Diet: Insectivore
  • Size: 5-6 inches tall
  • Weight: 0.9-1.1 ounces
  • Life span: 2-5 years
  • Habitat: Shrublands, woodlands
  • Found: Europe, Asia, Africa
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Nightingales often sing at dawn and dusk as well as at night.

Numbat

Small Australian marsupials that eat termites.

  • Species: Myrmecobius fasciatus
  • Diet: Insectivore
  • Size: 10-12 inches long
  • Weight: 10-18 ounces
  • Life span: 5-6 years
  • Habitat: Dry woodlands
  • Found: Western Australia
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Numbats have up to 50,000 termites in their stomachs at one time.

Needlefish

Slender fish with long jaws filled with needle-like teeth.

  • Species: Belonidae
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 12-48 inches long
  • Weight: 1-6 lbs
  • Life span: 3-5 years
  • Habitat: Coastal waters
  • Found: Worldwide
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Needlefish can leap out of the water and sometimes hit low-flying birds.

Nudibranch

Colorful sea slugs found on reefs and rocky shores.

  • Species: Nudibranchia
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 0.4-12 inches long
  • Weight: 0.1-2 ounces
  • Life span: 1-2 years
  • Habitat: Oceans, reefs
  • Found: Worldwide oceans
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Many nudibranchs store stinging cells from prey for their own defense.
