Kangaroo

Large marsupials from Australia that hop on powerful hind legs.

  • Species: Macropus rufus
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Size: 4-6 ft tall
  • Weight: 120-200 lbs
  • Life span: 8-12 years
  • Habitat: Grasslands, forests
  • Found: Australia
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Kangaroos can cover up to 25 feet in a single leap.

Koala

Tree-dwelling marsupials that eat eucalyptus leaves.

  • Species: Phascolarctos cinereus
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Size: 2-3 ft long
  • Weight: 9-33 lbs
  • Life span: 10-15 years
  • Habitat: Eucalyptus forests
  • Found: Australia
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Koalas sleep up to 18-22 hours a day.

Kingfisher

Brightly colored birds that dive for fish from perches above water.

  • Species: Alcedo atthis
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 6-13 inches long
  • Weight: 1-4 oz
  • Life span: 6-10 years
  • Habitat: Rivers, lakes, coasts
  • Found: Worldwide
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Kingfishers can see fish underwater and adjust for light refraction when diving.

Komodo Dragon

The world’s largest lizard, a fierce predator found on Indonesian islands.

  • Species: Varanus komodoensis
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 8-10 ft long
  • Weight: 150-300 lbs
  • Life span: 30 years
  • Habitat: Forests, savannas
  • Found: Indonesia
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Komodo dragons have venomous saliva and can take down prey much larger than themselves.

Kudu

Large African antelopes with impressive spiral horns.

  • Species: Tragelaphus strepsiceros
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Size: 4-5 ft tall
  • Weight: 330-600 lbs
  • Life span: 7-10 years
  • Habitat: Woodlands, savannas
  • Found: Africa
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Kudu horns can grow up to 6 feet long in a spiral shape.

Kiwi

Flightless birds native to New Zealand with long beaks and shy habits.

  • Species: Apteryx
  • Diet: Omnivore
  • Size: 14-20 inches tall
  • Weight: 2-9 lbs
  • Life span: 20-30 years
  • Habitat: Forests
  • Found: New Zealand
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Kiwi lay eggs that are huge relative to their body size.
