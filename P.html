Pig

Smart farm animals raised for meat and companionship.

  • Species: Sus scrofa domesticus
  • Diet: Omnivore
  • Size: 2-4 ft tall
  • Weight: 150-700 lbs
  • Life span: 10-15 years
  • Habitat: Farms
  • Found: Worldwide
  • Can be pet: Sometimes

Trivia: Pigs are highly social and can learn tricks like dogs.

Panda

Bamboo-eating bears with a distinctive black-and-white coat.

  • Species: Ailuropoda melanoleuca
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Size: 2-3 ft tall
  • Weight: 220-330 lbs
  • Life span: 20-30 years
  • Habitat: Bamboo forests
  • Found: China
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Pandas spend up to 14 hours a day eating bamboo.

Parrot

Colorful birds known for mimicking sounds and talking.

  • Species: Psittaciformes
  • Diet: Omnivore
  • Size: 4-40 inches tall
  • Weight: 1-60 oz
  • Life span: 10-80 years
  • Habitat: Rainforests, woodlands
  • Found: Worldwide tropics
  • Can be pet: Yes

Trivia: Some parrots can learn hundreds of words and phrases.

Penguin

Flightless seabirds adapted to swimming in cold waters.

  • Species: Spheniscidae
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 16-45 inches tall
  • Weight: 6-90 lbs
  • Life span: 15-20 years
  • Habitat: Coastal regions
  • Found: Southern Hemisphere
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Penguins can hold their breath for up to 20 minutes underwater.

Panther

A melanistic big cat, often a jaguar or leopard with black fur.

  • Species: Panthera
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 2-3 ft tall
  • Weight: 80-220 lbs
  • Life span: 12-15 years
  • Habitat: Forests, swamps
  • Found: Americas, Asia, Africa
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Panthers are known for their amazing night vision and stealth.

Peacock

Male peafowl with large iridescent tail feathers used in courtship.

  • Species: Pavo cristatus
  • Diet: Omnivore
  • Size: 3-5 ft long
  • Weight: 6-13 lbs
  • Life span: 15-20 years
  • Habitat: Forest edges, farms
  • Found: South Asia
  • Can be pet: Sometimes

Trivia: A peacock’s tail can have more than 150 eye-patterned feathers.
