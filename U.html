Urchin

Spiny marine animals that cling to rocks and reefs.

  • Species: Echinoidea
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Size: 1-4 inches across
  • Weight: 1-5 ounces
  • Life span: 20-30 years
  • Habitat: Oceans, rocky reefs
  • Found: Worldwide
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Sea urchins move with hundreds of tiny tube feet on their undersides.

Uakari

Red-faced monkeys native to the Amazon rainforest.

  • Species: Cacajao
  • Diet: Frugivore
  • Size: 17-24 inches tall
  • Weight: 7-9 lbs
  • Life span: 15-20 years
  • Habitat: Flooded forests
  • Found: South America
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Uakaris have bright red faces from visible blood vessels.

Umbrellabird

Tropical birds with a distinctive crest and deep calls.

  • Species: Cephalopterus
  • Diet: Frugivore
  • Size: 18-24 inches tall
  • Weight: 1.5-2.5 lbs
  • Life span: 10-15 years
  • Habitat: Rainforests
  • Found: Central and South America
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Umbrellabirds' head crests resemble an umbrella when raised.

Urial

Wild sheep with large curved horns from Central Asia.

  • Species: Ovis orientalis vignei
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Size: 3-4 ft tall
  • Weight: 120-220 lbs
  • Life span: 10-15 years
  • Habitat: Mountains, steppes
  • Found: Central Asia
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Urials are excellent climbers on rocky slopes.

Uromastyx

Spiny-tailed lizards adapted to hot desert climates.

  • Species: Uromastyx
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Size: 8-24 inches long
  • Weight: 1-6 lbs
  • Life span: 15-20 years
  • Habitat: Deserts
  • Found: Africa, Middle East, Asia
  • Can be pet: Sometimes

Trivia: Uromastyx use their spiny tail as a defensive whip.

Unicornfish

Tropical reef fish known for a horn-like forehead projection.

  • Species: Naso
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Size: 1-3 ft long
  • Weight: 5-20 lbs
  • Life span: 8-15 years
  • Habitat: Coral reefs
  • Found: Indo-Pacific
  • Can be pet: Sometimes

Trivia: Unicornfish use their horn-like bump for display and species recognition.
