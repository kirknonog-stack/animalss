Yak

Long-haired bovines from the Himalayan region.

  • Species: Bos grunniens
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Size: 5-6 ft tall
  • Weight: 600-1,200 lbs
  • Life span: 20-25 years
  • Habitat: Alpine meadows
  • Found: Tibet, Mongolia
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Yaks are adapted to high altitudes with thick fur and large lungs.

Yellowfin Tuna

Fast oceanic fish prized by anglers and chefs.

  • Species: Thunnus albacares
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 3-7 ft long
  • Weight: 100-400 lbs
  • Life span: 6-7 years
  • Habitat: Open ocean
  • Found: Tropical and subtropical seas
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Yellowfin tuna are strong swimmers and can migrate thousands of miles.

Yellowjacket

Aggressive wasps with bright yellow and black stripes.

  • Species: Vespula
  • Diet: Omnivore
  • Size: 0.4-0.8 inches long
  • Weight: Less than 1 ounce
  • Life span: 3-6 weeks
  • Habitat: Meadows, forests, urban areas
  • Found: North America
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Yellowjackets defend their nests aggressively when disturbed.

Yorkie (dog)

Tiny terrier breed known for its silky coat and bold personality.

  • Species: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Diet: Omnivore
  • Size: 6-9 inches tall
  • Weight: 4-7 lbs
  • Life span: 13-16 years
  • Habitat: Homes
  • Found: Worldwide
  • Can be pet: Yes

Trivia: Yorkies were originally bred to catch rats in textile mills.

Yabby

Freshwater crayfish found in Australia.

  • Species: Cherax destructor
  • Diet: Omnivore
  • Size: 4-6 inches long
  • Weight: Few ounces
  • Life span: 3-5 years
  • Habitat: Ponds, streams
  • Found: Australia
  • Can be pet: Yes

Trivia: Yabbies burrow into mud and can survive dry spells underground.

Yeti Crab

Deep-sea crustaceans with hairy claws found near hydrothermal vents.

  • Species: Kiwa
  • Diet: Chemosynthetic bacteria
  • Size: 4-6 inches long
  • Weight: Few ounces
  • Life span: Unknown
  • Habitat: Deep ocean vents
  • Found: Pacific Ocean
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Yeti crabs farm bacteria on their hairy claws to feed themselves.
