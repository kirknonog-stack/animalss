Aardvark

Nocturnal insectivore from sub-Saharan Africa.

  • Species: Orycteropus afer
  • Diet: Ants & termites
  • Size: 3.3-4.9 ft
  • Weight: 88-143 lbs
  • Life span: 10-23 years
  • Habitat: Woodlands, grasslands
  • Found: Sub-Saharan Africa
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Aardvarks can eat up to 50,000 ants in one night.

Ant

A tiny insect known for strength and social teamwork.

  • Species: Formicidae
  • Diet: Omnivore
  • Size: 0.1-1.0 in
  • Weight: 0.0001-0.5 oz
  • Life span: 4-7 weeks
  • Habitat: Forests, grasslands
  • Found: Worldwide
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Ants can carry many times their own body weight.

Alligator

A large reptile found in freshwater swamps and rivers.

  • Species: Alligator mississippiensis
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 10-15 ft
  • Weight: 500-1,000 lbs
  • Life span: 35-50 years
  • Habitat: Freshwater marshes, rivers
  • Found: Southeastern United States
  • Can be pet: Rarely

Trivia: Their powerful bite is one of the strongest in the animal kingdom.

Armadillo

Armor-plated mammal that rolls into a protective ball.

  • Species: Dasypus novemcinctus
  • Diet: Omnivore
  • Size: 15-59 inches
  • Weight: 3.5-17.5 lbs
  • Life span: 4-15 years
  • Habitat: Grasslands, forests
  • Found: Americas
  • Can be pet: Rarely

Trivia: Armadillos can hold their breath for up to six minutes while swimming.

Alpaca

Domesticated camelid raised for soft fleece.

  • Species: Vicugna pacos
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Size: 3-4.5 ft tall
  • Weight: 100-200 lbs
  • Life span: 15-20 years
  • Habitat: Highlands, farms
  • Found: South America
  • Can be pet: Yes

Trivia: Alpaca fleece is warmer and softer than sheep wool.

Axolotl

A salamander known for regenerating limbs.

  • Species: Ambystoma mexicanum
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 6-12 inches
  • Weight: 8-20 oz
  • Life span: 10-15 years
  • Habitat: Freshwater lakes
  • Found: Mexico
  • Can be pet: Yes

Trivia: Axolotls remain juvenile throughout life, a trait called neoteny.
