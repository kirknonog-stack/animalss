Owl

Nocturnal birds of prey with excellent night vision.

  • Species: Strigiformes
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 6-28 inches tall
  • Weight: 1-10 lbs
  • Life span: 5-25 years
  • Habitat: Forests, grasslands
  • Found: Worldwide
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Owls can rotate their heads about 270 degrees.

Otter

Playful aquatic mammals known for using tools.

  • Species: Mustelidae
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 1.5-4.5 ft long
  • Weight: 11-100 lbs
  • Life span: 10-15 years
  • Habitat: Rivers, coasts
  • Found: Americas, Europe, Asia, Africa
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Sea otters hold hands while sleeping to stay together.

Octopus

Intelligent cephalopods with eight flexible arms.

  • Species: Octopoda
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 1-16 ft across
  • Weight: 2-110 lbs
  • Life span: 1-5 years
  • Habitat: Oceans, reefs
  • Found: Worldwide
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Octopuses can squeeze through openings smaller than their bodies.

Ox

Domesticated cattle used for heavy farm work and hauling.

  • Species: Bos taurus
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Size: 4.5-5.5 ft tall
  • Weight: 1,200-2,000 lbs
  • Life span: 15-20 years
  • Habitat: Farms, fields
  • Found: Worldwide
  • Can be pet: Sometimes

Trivia: Oxen are usually castrated male cattle trained for work.

Ocelot

Medium-sized wild cats with spotted coats.

  • Species: Leopardus pardalis
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 20-40 inches long
  • Weight: 18-40 lbs
  • Life span: 7-10 years
  • Habitat: Forests, grasslands
  • Found: Americas
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Ocelots are excellent climbers and swimmers.

Orangutan

Intelligent tree-dwelling apes from Asian rainforests.

  • Species: Pongo
  • Diet: Omnivore
  • Size: 4-5 ft tall
  • Weight: 75-200 lbs
  • Life span: 30-40 years
  • Habitat: Rainforests
  • Found: Borneo, Sumatra
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Orangutans are among the most intelligent primates and use tools in the wild.
