Wolf

Pack-hunting canines that live in diverse habitats.

  • Species: Canis lupus
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 2-3 ft tall
  • Weight: 50-110 lbs
  • Life span: 6-8 years
  • Habitat: Forests, tundra
  • Found: North America, Eurasia
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Wolves communicate with howls that can travel miles.

Whale

Massive marine mammals that range from coastal to deep ocean waters.

  • Species: Cetacea
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 10-100 ft long
  • Weight: 2-200 tons
  • Life span: 30-90 years
  • Habitat: Oceans
  • Found: Worldwide
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Whales use sound to navigate and communicate across long distances.

Wombat

Burrowing marsupials from Australia with strong legs.

  • Species: Vombatidae
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Size: 2-3 ft long
  • Weight: 40-80 lbs
  • Life span: 15 years
  • Habitat: Forests, grasslands
  • Found: Australia
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Wombats have cube-shaped droppings that help mark territory.

Weasel

Small carnivores with slender bodies and quick movements.

  • Species: Mustela
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 6-16 inches long
  • Weight: 2-10 ounces
  • Life span: 3-5 years
  • Habitat: Fields, forests
  • Found: Worldwide
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Weasels can squeeze through tiny holes to hunt prey.

Walrus

Large Arctic mammals with tusks and whiskers.

  • Species: Odobenus rosmarus
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 8-12 ft long
  • Weight: 1,000-3,700 lbs
  • Life span: 20-30 years
  • Habitat: Arctic seas
  • Found: Arctic regions
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Walruses use their tusks to haul themselves onto ice and to fight.

Woodpecker

Birds that peck at wood to find insects and build nests.

  • Species: Picidae
  • Diet: Insectivore
  • Size: 6-20 inches long
  • Weight: 1-14 ounces
  • Life span: 4-11 years
  • Habitat: Forests, woodlands
  • Found: Worldwide
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Woodpeckers have specially adapted skulls to absorb pecking impact.
