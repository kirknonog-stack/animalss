Vulture

Scavenging birds that help keep ecosystems clean.

  • Species: Accipitridae
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 2-5 ft wingspan
  • Weight: 5-30 lbs
  • Life span: 10-30 years
  • Habitat: Plains, deserts, mountains
  • Found: Worldwide
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Vultures can smell carrion from miles away.

Viper

Venomous snakes with hinged fangs for striking prey.

  • Species: Viperidae
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 16 inches-8 ft
  • Weight: 1-30 lbs
  • Life span: 10-20 years
  • Habitat: Forests, deserts
  • Found: Europe, Asia, Africa, Americas
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Vipers strike quickly and often release venom through hollow fangs.

Vicuna

Wild South American camelids prized for their fine wool.

  • Species: Vicugna vicugna
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Size: 3-4 ft tall
  • Weight: 88-120 lbs
  • Life span: 15-20 years
  • Habitat: Highland plains
  • Found: Andes
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Vicuna wool is one of the most expensive natural fibers.

Vampire Bat

Small bats that feed on the blood of other animals.

  • Species: Desmodus rotundus
  • Diet: Hematophagous
  • Size: 3-4 inches long
  • Weight: 2-3 ounces
  • Life span: 9-12 years
  • Habitat: Caves, hollow trees
  • Found: Americas
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Vampire bats use heat sensors to find blood-rich skin.

Velvet Worm

Soft-bodied invertebrates that hunt with sticky slime.

  • Species: Onychophora
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 0.5-8 inches long
  • Weight: 0.1-2 ounces
  • Life span: 5-10 years
  • Habitat: Leaf litter, forests
  • Found: Tropics
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Velvet worms shoot sticky slime to trap prey.

Vervet Monkey

Small African monkeys with striking facial markings.

  • Species: Chlorocebus pygerythrus
  • Diet: Omnivore
  • Size: 1.5-2 ft tall
  • Weight: 6-12 lbs
  • Life span: 12-20 years
  • Habitat: Savannah, woodlands
  • Found: Africa
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Vervet monkeys communicate with a variety of alarm calls.
