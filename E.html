Elephant

The largest land mammal with a trunk and tusks.

  • Species: Loxodonta africana
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Size: 8-13 ft tall
  • Weight: 5,000-14,000 lbs
  • Life span: 60-70 years
  • Habitat: Grasslands, forests, savannas
  • Found: Africa, Asia
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Elephants use their trunks for breathing, drinking, and grabbing objects.

Eagle

Powerful birds of prey with keen eyesight and strong talons.

  • Species: Accipitridae family
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 2-3 ft tall
  • Weight: 6-15 lbs
  • Life span: 20-30 years
  • Habitat: Mountains, forests, coasts
  • Found: Worldwide
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Eagles can spot prey from over a mile away.

Eel

Long, snake-like fish that live in both fresh and salt water.

  • Species: Anguilliformes order
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 1-4 ft long
  • Weight: 1-20 lbs
  • Life span: 10-20 years
  • Habitat: Rivers, oceans, reefs
  • Found: Worldwide
  • Can be pet: Sometimes

Trivia: Some eels can generate electric shocks to stun prey.

Emu

Large flightless birds native to Australia.

  • Species: Dromaius novaehollandiae
  • Diet: Omnivore
  • Size: 5-6 ft tall
  • Weight: 90-130 lbs
  • Life span: 10-20 years
  • Habitat: Open forests, grasslands
  • Found: Australia
  • Can be pet: Sometimes

Trivia: Emus can sprint up to 30 mph with long, powerful legs.

Elk

Large deer species famous for their impressive antlers and bugling calls.

  • Species: Cervus canadensis
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Size: 4.5-5 ft tall
  • Weight: 500-700 lbs
  • Life span: 10-20 years
  • Habitat: Forests, grasslands, mountains
  • Found: North America, Asia
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Elk males make loud bugling calls during mating season.

Earthworm

Soil-dwelling worms that help enrich and aerate the ground.

  • Species: Lumbricidae family
  • Diet: Detritivore
  • Size: 2-14 inches long
  • Weight: 0.1-0.2 oz
  • Life span: 4-8 years
  • Habitat: Soil, compost, gardens
  • Found: Worldwide
  • Can be pet: Sometimes

Trivia: Earthworms can consume their body weight in soil each day.
