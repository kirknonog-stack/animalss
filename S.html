Snake

Legless reptiles that slither through forests and deserts.

  • Species: Serpentes
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 1-20 ft long
  • Weight: 0.5-200 lbs
  • Life span: 9-25 years
  • Habitat: Forests, grasslands, deserts
  • Found: Worldwide
  • Can be pet: Sometimes

Trivia: Some snakes can detect heat from prey using specialized pit organs.

Sheep

Domesticated woolly mammals raised for wool and meat.

  • Species: Ovis aries
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Size: 2-4 ft tall
  • Weight: 100-350 lbs
  • Life span: 10-12 years
  • Habitat: Farms, grasslands
  • Found: Worldwide
  • Can be pet: Sometimes

Trivia: Sheep are very social and form strong flock bonds.

Shark

Ancient predatory fish found throughout the world’s oceans.

  • Species: Selachimorpha
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 1-40 ft long
  • Weight: 5-20,000 lbs
  • Life span: 20-30 years
  • Habitat: Oceans
  • Found: Worldwide
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Sharks can detect electromagnetic signals from other animals.

Seal

Marine mammals with flippers adapted for swimming and lounging on ice.

  • Species: Pinnipedia
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 3-8 ft long
  • Weight: 100-900 lbs
  • Life span: 20-30 years
  • Habitat: Coastal waters
  • Found: Worldwide
  • Can be pet: No

Trivia: Seals can hold their breath underwater for up to 30 minutes.

Spider

Eight-legged arachnids that spin silk webs for hunting.

  • Species: Araneae
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 0.05-12 inches
  • Weight: 0.01-2 ounces
  • Life span: 1-25 years
  • Habitat: Homes, forests, grasslands
  • Found: Worldwide
  • Can be pet: Sometimes

Trivia: Spiders can produce multiple types of silk for different purposes.

Swan

Large waterfowl with graceful necks and white plumage.

  • Species: Cygnus
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Size: 4-5 ft long
  • Weight: 15-30 lbs
  • Life span: 10-20 years
  • Habitat: Lakes, rivers
  • Found: Europe, Asia, North America
  • Can be pet: Sometimes

Trivia: Swans mate for life and often swim in graceful pairs.
